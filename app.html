Help


Follow the following steps to convert RG to NFA:
  1. Input Regular Grammars
    • Enter your regular grammar using the format "A->0B|1C" line by line with no empty lines and no spaces within.
    • Must enter minimum 3 alphabets and 1 epsilon (ε).
  2. Convert Regular Grammars to NFA
    • Click on the 'Convert RG to NFA' button to generate NFA information and output transition tables for your grammars.
  3. Check Input Strings Acceptance
    • Input your test strings line by line with no empty lines in the 'Check Strings (Input)' textarea.
    • Click on the 'Check' button to check the acceptance of the strings. The acceptance result will output below.
  4. Clear Input
    • To clear all inputs and test strings, click on the 'Clear' button.
